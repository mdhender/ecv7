# Turn Processing

Each turn is processed in stages, as shown below.
 
---

## 1. Mining and Farming Production

Mining and farming production is calculated.

## 2. Manufacturing Production

Manufacturing production is calculated.

## 3. Combat

Combat takes place.

## 4. Set Up Orders

Set up orders are processed.

## 5. Dis-assembly Orders

Dis-assembly orders are processed.

## 6. Build Change Orders

Build change orders are entered.

## 7. Mining Change Orders

Mining change orders are entered.

## 8. Transfers

Transfers are processed.

## 9. Assembly Orders

Assembly orders are processed.

## 10. Market and Trade Station Activity

All market and trade station activity takes place.

## 11. Surveys

Surveys are carried out.

## 12. Probe and Sensor Reports

Probe and sensor reports are compiled.

## 13. Espionage

Espionage activity takes place.

## 14. Ship Movement

Ship movement occurs.

## 15. Draft Orders

Draft orders are processed.

## 16. Pay and Ration Orders

Pay and ration orders are entered.

## 17. Rebellion

Rebellion occurs.

## 18. Rebel Increases

Rebel increases take place.

## 19. Naming and Control Orders

Naming and control orders are processed.

## 20. Population Increases

Population increases are calculated.

## 21. News Service Reports

News service reports are compiled.
