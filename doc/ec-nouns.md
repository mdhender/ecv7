# Epimethean Challenge — Noun Extraction

Items that empires **produce**, **control**, or **exchange**: all nouns extracted
from the text, normalized to singular lowercase. Organized by semantic category.

---

## Natural Resources
*(mined from deposits; bought and sold at markets)*

| Noun | Notes |
|------|-------|
| fuel | mined; used for production, transport, combat |
| gold | mined; primary currency for all trade |
| metallic | mined; raw material input for factories |
| non-metallic | mined; raw material input for factories |

## Purposefully Excluded

These nouns appear frequently in the text but are **not** items that empires
produce, control, or exchange in the unit/commodity sense.

| Noun | Reason excluded |
|------|-----------------|
| factory group | logical grouping of factory units, not a unit itself |
| mine group | logical grouping of mine units, not a unit itself |
| orbit | geographic location; ships and colonies occupy orbits but orbits are not exchanged |
| planet | geographic location; controlled but not produced or traded |
| system | geographic location (solar system); explored and controlled but not exchanged |
| race | faction identifier; not a producible or tradeable item |

---

## Notes

- **Gold** appears in two categories because it is both a mined natural resource
  and the game's medium of exchange.
- **Rebel** is included because it is listed as a unit type in Population Chart A/B,
  even though it is described as a tally rather than a physical unit.
- **Research point** is an implicit unit: factories ordered to do research produce
  "one research point per factory unit (times its TL) per year."
- Units sold on the market include all manufactured items; the text explicitly
  shows `structural`, `space drive`, and `TL` (technology level) as traded examples.
